Who We Are

Where the road
meets the table

Argentina's racing soul and its kitchens — on one private journey.

Torque & Taste is a Buenos Aires–based travel experience company dedicated to the intersection of Argentina's extraordinary automotive heritage and its world-class gastronomy and wine culture.

Every circuit is private, fully curated, and designed for the discerning traveler who wants more than a museum ticket — they want the story, the road, and the flavor that comes with it.
